Trigger: You have a multi-stage process A→B→C→D

Transformation: What happens if you start with B, C, or D?

Test-driven development is my favorite example of this Thinkie. Everybody codes & then tests? What if we test & then code?

The Half Wavelength Shift will test your skill at deferring judgement. A comes before B, C, & D for good reasons. Setting those reasons aside for a moment is likely to be hard.

The seeds of the Half Wavelength Shift were planted when my dad let me play with his oscilloscope & wave generator. I got used to seeing sine waves & then watching them shift horizontally. Just because you have a waveform, that doesn’t mean that it’s immutable.
