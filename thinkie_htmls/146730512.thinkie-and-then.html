Pattern: You have a potential decision to evaluate.

Transformation: Ask, “…and then?”

“We need more productivity. Let’s set higher goals for the number of lines of code programmers write per day.”

We all know how this ends. And yet, and yet, I have personally witnessed the introduction of line counts to performance evaluation at a large, highly respected engineering organization. In the last decade.

I don’t know what it is that keeps folks from following up on the consequences of their proposals, even when prediction is easy. Now, granted, sometimes it’s hard or impossible to guess unintended consequences. But sometimes it’s dead easy & folks still don’t do it.

So ask, “…and then?”, “…and then?”, “…and then?”
