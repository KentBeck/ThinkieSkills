Pattern: a conversation has gotten stuck short of resolution but the conversation remains promising.

Transformation: summarize the other party’s position in a way they would agree with.

It’s natural to judge as I go along. I’m having a conversation with someone. I’m thinking, yeah, I don’t think you grasp the whole situation. But maybe I’m missing something.

All that judgement interferes with actually listening. Often I’ll have wide areas of agreement with someone but sitting in judgement keeps me from seeing that we’re actually close.

Thinkie Your Position is a way to suspend judgement for a moment, to encourage listening fully, to reconnect with my conversational partner. “Let me see if I’m hearing you accurately…”

Their response to the summary provides missing information.

  • You missed something important. Now you know & you can re-engage judgement with new information.

  • You really did understand. Now you can re-engage judgement with greater confidence.
