Pattern: you’re having trouble communicating with someone.

Transformation: invite them to look at the situation from your perspective.

I know, I know. This is the opposite of the usual advice to look at the situation from the other person’s perspective. It’s a Thinkie. It generates ideas, not good ideas.

I’ve used this when evaluating investment opportunities. The entrepreneur is telling me all about their cool technology, what amazing uses people will put it to. I’m not convinced.

I could just say “no” at this point. Thinkie My Perspective says instead to offer them my point of view, to give them a chance to speak more persuasively. “Here’s how it looks to me. I have enough capital to make 2 more investments. I have 8 candidates. I need to see some concrete path to revenue before I can say yes. Tell me that story—how are you going to earn revenue?”

[I suppose there must also be a Thinkie Your Position where you restate what you’ve heard them say without judgement as preparation for evaluation.]
