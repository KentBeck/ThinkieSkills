Pattern: you have a bad situation & it’s getting worse.

Transformation: trace the effects. Find the loop where more of the bad is causing more of the bad. Go back one or two steps in the cycle & influence that effect the opposite direction.

This is one of the most exciting Thinkies for me, because it offers tremendous leverage in a seemingly-hopeless situation. As soon as I find the reinforcing loop, I have all the influence I need to start things getting better.

(The way I learned systems thinking this was called a “positive feedback loop”. Over & over I found myself saying, “Not ‘positive’ in the sense of ‘good’, ‘positive’ in the sense of ‘more creates more’.” I got tired of the confusion so I started using “reinforcing loop” for a loop where more of an effect tends to create more of an effect & “inhibiting loop” for a loop where more of an effect tends to create less of that effect.)
