Thinkie Explore or Extract is based on the multi-armed bandit problem. Given a bank of slot machines with different-and-unknown payouts, how do you decide in which machine to place your next quarter?

The provably optimal algorithm is:

  1. Start with equal estimated payouts

  2. Loop:

    1. Simulate putting a quarter into all machines.

    2. Put the quarter into the machine with the highest simulated payout.

    3. Update your estimated payout for the machine based on the actual payout.

That’s it. Each round of the game produces both:

  • The payout of a machine &

  • Better information about future payouts.
