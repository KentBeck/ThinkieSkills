“Many a true word hath been spoken in jest.”
―William Shakespeare, King Lear

The next Thinkie in the language section is Laughter. Come to think of it, it’s not so much a language game as a social game. Whatever, the classification scheme is still evolving.

Pattern: A statement in conversation is followed by nervous laughter.

Transformation: Take the statement seriously.

Example: “If we could just throw away all the source code & start over we could certainly succeed.” Maybe so. Time to evaluate.

Now this is maybe part of the magic of Thinkies for me. Defer judgement a minute. What would it look like to throw away all the source code? What would be required? Who would win? Who would lose? Okay, now what do you think of the idea?

Thinkies are a way of generating lots of ideas, not generating good ideas. And Thinkies generate lots of ideas that other folks seem to miss. And every once in a while one of them is good.

Again, try it. Note when you try it. Share with a friend. Maybe it’ll become a habit.
