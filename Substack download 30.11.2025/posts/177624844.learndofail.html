Pass or Fail! Are those really the only two options when Exploring?

I visited a startup incubator the other day. Every week every team had to present their progress at the end of which they gave themselves a Pass or a Fail.

I watched team after team talk about everything they learned over the course of the week, and then, eyes downcast, judged themselves to have Failed because they didn’t accomplish what they had set out to accomplish.

Accountability

I’m pro-accountability. In The Forest (XP) we develop transparently. Tests, planning, continuous integration, even the furniture layout if we are sitting together are all tuned so there are no private, hidden decisions.
