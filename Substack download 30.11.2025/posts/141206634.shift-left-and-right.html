Trigger: You have a multi-stage process with handoffs & you’d like to eliminate a handoff

Transformation: Look at the value realized by person handed off to. What would it take for someone else to realize that value earlier or later in the process?

The classic example is the relationship between developers & testers. (Note that this picture ignores the cost of queueing delay at every handoff.) One optimization would be for the tester to write automated tests for every defect they find, resulting in one less handoff.

The cost of automating the test might be higher than the cost of testing manually twice but the result could still be a net gain for the system overall.

Once again, this Thinkie is a way to generate ideas, not good ideas. Once you’ve generated the idea for moving work earlier or later in the process you’ll need to evaluate whether its a net win or loss for the process.
