McDonald’s is part of the series of Decision Thinkies.

Trigger: You sense that a group may be ready to make a decision but is stalling.

Transformation: You propose a bad-but-not-ludicrous alternative.

This one’s called “McDonald’s” because the classic situation is going out to eat. Where are we going? Oh I don’t know. How about McDonald’s? Oh hell no, let’s go to XXX.

This Thinkie is related to Naivety (will write about this one soon). Ideally the sub-par decision you propose would be acceptable if the group chose it.
