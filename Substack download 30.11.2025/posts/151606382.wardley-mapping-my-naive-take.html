Investment

We’re in some situation. We have limited capital. We want return. Of all the things we could invest in, which should we invest in & which should we ignore? When we invest, what should our risk stack look like to improve outcomes?

The fundamental questions to be answered in Wardley Mapping are:

  1. What should we invest in?

  2. In what way should we invest? (Build versus buy, for example, or explore-style versus extract-style.)

We could do an engineer thing, make a list of all the investment opportunities, sort it by ROI, and go down the list until we run out of money, but there’s so much information missing from that process that we know we need more.

  • What are obvious investment opportunities?

  • What are hidden investment opportunities that we’ll have to dig for?

  • How do opportunities support/interfere with each other?

  • Which opportunities offer what kinds of leverage?
